The Problem Every Finance Team Knows

Bank reconciliation is one of the most time-consuming tasks in any finance department. For businesses with multiple bank accounts, the problem compounds — each account means another round of tedious manual work.

❌

Manual bank reconciliation takes hours. Download the statement, open Opera, and start typing. Every transaction entered by hand — 50, 80, sometimes 100+ entries per statement. Typos are inevitable. Transposed digits cost you even more time to find.

❌

Bank accounts are never up to date. If reconciliation takes hours, it gets put off. Statements pile up. The cashbook in Opera falls behind. Business owners cannot see the real cash position because the books lag weeks behind reality.

❌

Cashflow picture is unclear. If you do not know your actual bank balance in Opera, you cannot make confident decisions about spending, investment, or supplier payments. You end up logging into the bank portal instead of trusting your own accounting system.

❌

Data entry errors create more work. A wrong amount, a misidentified customer, a receipt posted as a payment — these errors ripple through the ledger. Finding them at month end costs more time than the original entry.

❌

Reconciliation falls behind. When it takes 2-3 hours per bank account, it is tempting to skip a month. Then two months. Then you are faced with a backlog that takes an entire day to clear — and that is a day your team is not doing anything else.

The Real Cost

A business with 3-4 bank accounts can easily spend 8-12 hours per month on bank reconciliation alone. That is one and a half working days every month spent on a task that adds no strategic value — just keeping the books accurate enough to trust.

Security & Control

Automated does not mean uncontrolled. Every step includes rigorous validation, and nothing posts without your approval.

🔒

Statement Validation

Sort code and account number verified against Opera bank configuration. Opening balance checked to the penny against Opera's reconciled balance. Wrong statement? Rejected instantly. Duplicate? Blocked automatically.

📋

Full Audit Trail

Every transaction carries the bank statement reference. Your auditors can trace any cashbook entry back to the original bank statement line. Complete double-entry with nominal ledger postings, transfer files, and period balances.

🔄

Sequential Import

Statements must be imported in chronological order. You cannot skip a month or process out of sequence. Opening balance of each statement must match the closing balance of the previous one. No gaps in the audit trail.

⚠

Duplicate Prevention

Every statement is tracked. Once imported, the same statement cannot be imported again — even by a different user. Transactions already in Opera are identified before import. No risk of double-posting.

🗃

Statement Archive

Every imported statement is stored and retrievable. View the original PDF alongside the Opera postings at any time. Complete history of every reconciliation, accessible whenever you need it.
