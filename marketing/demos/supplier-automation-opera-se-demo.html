Smart Business Rules

The system applies intelligent business logic to every reconciliation difference. This is a finance system - figures must be accurate, and the rules protect your cash position.

Query (Not In Your Favour)

  • ● Invoice on their statement, not in our system
  • ● Their amount higher than ours
  • ● Credit they claim sent, we don't have
  • ● Payment they claim made, we haven't received

Stay Quiet (In Your Favour)

  • ● Overpayment from supplier
  • ● Credit we have that they forgot
  • ● Their amount lower than ours
  • ● Invoice we have that they haven't billed

Always Notify (Helpful)

  • ● Payments we have made not on their statement
  • ● Payment forecast with due dates
  • ● Next payment run date

Flag for Review

  • ● Large discrepancies above threshold
  • ● Unusual patterns

Result: Protected Cash Position

By only querying items not in your favour, the system ensures you never accidentally remind a supplier about money they owe you or credits they have forgotten to apply.
