Overview

Statement Reconciliation helps you compare supplier statements against your Purchase Ledger to identify missing invoices, payments, and discrepancies.

Key Features

  • Extract transactions from supplier statement emails (PDF/text)
  • Automatic matching against Purchase Ledger transactions
  • Identify missing invoices and unrecorded payments
  • Highlight timing differences and disputed items
  • Generate variance reports for review
  • Support for multiple statement formats
