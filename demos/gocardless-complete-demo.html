Automatic Fee & VAT Reclaim

GoCardless charges fees on every transaction - and those fees include reclaimable VAT. Without automation, many businesses miss this VAT reclaim entirely, effectively overpaying HMRC. Crakd.ai automatically separates the net fee from the VAT, posts to the correct nominal accounts, and creates the VAT return records that ensure you reclaim every penny you are owed.

Over a year, that is nearly £400 recovered automatically. The system creates both VAT analysis and VAT return tracking records, ensuring your next VAT return is accurate without any extra work.

The Control Bank - This Changes Everything

This is the feature that transforms GoCardless from a reconciliation headache into a solved problem. Without it, you have 18 cashbook entries that will never match the single line on your bank statement - reconciliation is effectively impossible. With the control bank, all the detail posts to a clearing account, and a single net transfer hits your actual bank. The result: one Opera entry, one bank statement line, perfect match. Every time.

Proper Accounting, Automatically

The control bank always nets to zero after each payout - receipts flow in, fees and the net transfer flow out, and the balance clears perfectly. This is exactly how professional clearing accounts should work, but configured for you automatically. All the detail is preserved in the control bank for audit purposes, while your main bank stays clean and reconcilable. No accounting knowledge required to use it.
